About Me

Hi there! I'm an AI researcher.

I've defended my PhD in November 2025, on the subject: "Analyzing binary programs and obfuscation with graph-based representations and machine learning".

My research topics include (but are not limited to): graphs & graph representation learning, with a focus on cybersecurity applications, especially reverse-engineering, obfuscation, binary analysis, program synthesis. But I'm also a big fan of more general AI research topics ; such as weak signals analysis, fusion of multi-types and complex data, anomaly detection, social networks analysis, fraud detection, intelligence, everything linked to drones, aircrafts and so on.

If you're interested to discuss on these topics, please reach out !
