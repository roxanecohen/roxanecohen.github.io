About Me

Hi there! I'm an AI researcher.

I defended my PhD in November 2025 on the topic: "Analyzing binary programs and obfuscation with graph-based representations and machine learning".

My research interests include (but are not limited to) graphs & graph representation learning, with a focus on cybersecurity applications, especially reverse-engineering, obfuscation, binary analysis and program synthesis. I'm also interest in broader AI topics, such as weak signal analysis, multimodal data fusion, anomaly detection, social network analysis, fraud detection, intelligence, as well as everything linked to defense in general, like drones, aircrafts and so on.

If you’re interested in discussing these topics, feel free to reach out!
